M4 thermal module

A Different Type of Heater

The space needs heat. A commercial spa runs sessions all day — the building needs to stay warm, the sauna cabin needs to reach temperature, hot water needs to flow. That energy has to come from somewhere.

The M4 is a direct replacement for a conventional electric heater at the same wattage. All electrical energy in is converted to heat energy out. It heats the building, the sauna cabin starts warm so the stove covers a smaller delta, and excess routes to hot water. The difference: it also mines bitcoin.

Same input. Dual output.
