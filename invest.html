LYN

A system of 7 modular components for outdoor saunas with integrated bitcoin mining. 2,100 numbered units. Unlimited bespoke indoor installations. Designed by Groundwork Studio. Manufactured by KOERNER Saunabau.

Unit prices range from €55,000 (outdoor) to €210,000 (bespoke indoor). Every installation runs an M4 thermal module — bitcoin mining hardware that converts electricity to heat while accumulating sats.

LYN is a bitcoin accumulation vehicle that currently focuses on designing 2,100 saunas.

Thesis

A self-funding showroom. Session revenue covers the operating cost. Every guest experiences the full product. The site targets 10 unit sales per year.

Every spa session is 75 minutes inside a working LYN installation — KOERNER cabin, M4 thermal system, the full design language. Every session is a product experience at €55–210k. Session revenue exists to make the showroom cost nothing to operate. Unit sales are the business.

Conversion

Conservative session volume: ~750–900 sessions/year. At up to 4 guests per session: 2,000–3,600 visitors annually. 10 sales from that base is a 0.3–0.5% conversion rate — on a €55–210k considered purchase the visitor has just physically experienced.


Customer acquisition cost

Alternative: trade fairs, ads, a sales team — €50–100k/year with no experiential conversion. Here, every paying spa guest is a warm lead who has already used the product. The site gets paid for its own marketing.
